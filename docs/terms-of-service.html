Services Description

Topeka Foundation Pros provides professional foundation repair and related services throughout Topeka, Kansas and surrounding communities in northeast Kansas. Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Foundation Repair: Comprehensive foundation stabilization and repair services for residential and commercial properties experiencing settling, cracking, or structural issues
  • Basement Waterproofing: Interior and exterior waterproofing solutions, drainage systems, sump pump installation, and moisture control
  • Foundation Crack Repair: Professional repair of foundation cracks using epoxy injection, polyurethane injection, and other proven methods
  • Pier Installation: Installation of steel piers, helical piers, and concrete piers to stabilize and support foundations
  • Crawl Space Repair: Crawl space encapsulation, support joist repair, and moisture control solutions
  • Concrete Leveling: Mudjacking and polyurethane foam injection to level sunken concrete slabs, sidewalks, and driveways
